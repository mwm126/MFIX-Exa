Qualitative Benchmarks¶

MFiX-Exa uses a three level approach to regression testing spaning from simple and/or short smoke tests to validation problems comparing against experimental data. Most of the validation benchmarks target the physics of interest to MFiX-Exa’s intended audience, i.e., dense bubbling, fast fluidization and pneumatic transport of particulate solids by a light gas. In an effort to widen the phase-space in which MFiX-Exa may be (potentially) applied, a set of qualitative benchmark problems are provided below which focus more on the phenomenology of a given, problem rather than averaged statistical measures. Animations of numerical soluitons to several of the qualitative benchmarks can be seen in the Gallery
